# Introduction

Qirvo is a personal life management platform that unifies planning, habits, health, tasks powered by AI-driven assistence where you need it most.

Qirvo will analyze your data, whenever you want and provide valuable insights into your life, including giving you some valuable advice on how to improve in areas that need it most.

## Getting Started

Get started by **creating a account** here: [Qirvo.ai](https://app.qirvo.ai/en/auth/register).

## Your Dashboard

Your Dashboard by default will have all modules active, including:

- [Daily Planner](/docs/user-guide/daily-planner)
- [Tasks & Goals](/docs/user-guide/task-goals)
- [Health Tracker](/docs/user-guide/health-tracker)
- [Bullet Journal](/docs/user-guide/journal)
- [Projects](/docs/user-guide/projects)
- [Echo CLI](/docs/user-guide/echo-cli)
- [AI Insights](/docs/user-guide/ai-insights)
- [Reminders](/docs/user-guide/reminders)
- [Settings](/docs/user-guide/settings)
- [Profile](/docs/user-guide/profile)
