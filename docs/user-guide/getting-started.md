### First Login

1. Navigate to the Dashboard [URL](https://app.qirvo.ai/en/)
2. Sign in with your registered email and password
3. Complete your profile setup if prompted
4. Explore the dashboard navigation menu on the left

### Dashboard Navigation

The left sidebar contains all your available modules:

- **Daily Planner**: Plan your day and conduct evening reviews
- **Tasks & Goals**: Manage tasks and track long-term goals
- **Health Tracker**: Monitor health metrics and symptoms
- **Bullet Journal**: Flexible note-taking and journaling
- **Projects**: Track development and personal projects
- **Echo CLI**: Command-line interface integration
- **AI Insights**: AI-powered analysis and recommendations
- **Reminders**: Schedule and manage notifications
- **Settings**: Customize your dashboard experience
- **Profile**: Manage your account information

## Teams & Collaboration

### Team Features (Team Subscription)

If you have a team subscription, additional features are available:

**Team Management:**

- Invite team members
- Assign roles and permissions
- Share projects and resources
- Collaborate on tasks and goals

**Team Admin Features:**

- Access to **Teams** section in navigation
- Manage team member accounts
- Control feature access for team members
- Monitor team activity and progress

**How to Use Teams:**

1. Navigate to **Teams** (team admins only)
2. Invite team members via email
3. Assign appropriate roles and permissions
4. Share relevant projects and resources
5. Monitor team collaboration and progress

### Team Member Experience

Team members have access to:

- Shared projects and resources
- Echo CLI for collaboration
- Personal dashboard features
- Limited administrative capabilities

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Login Problems:**

- Verify email and password are correct
- Check for email verification requirements
- Use password reset if needed
- Clear browser cache and cookies

**Feature Not Available:**

- Check module settings in Settings
- Verify subscription level includes the feature
- Contact support if feature should be available

**Performance Issues:**

- Clear browser cache
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Check internet connection
- Try a different browser

**Data Sync Issues:**

- Refresh the page
- Check internet connectivity
- Log out and log back in
- Contact support if data appears lost

### Getting Help

**In-App Support:**

- Check the help documentation within each module
- Use the search function to find specific features
- Review tooltips and guided tours

**Contact Support:**

- Use the support contact form
- Include specific error messages
- Describe steps to reproduce issues
- Mention your subscription type and browser

## Tips for Success

:::

### Getting the Most from Command Dashboard

1. **Start Small**: Begin with one or two modules and gradually add more
2. **Be Consistent**: Regular use leads to better insights and habits
3. **Customize**: Adjust settings and modules to fit your workflow
4. **Review Regularly**: Use insights and reports to improve your approach
5. **Integrate**: Connect Command Dashboard with your existing tools and workflows

### Best Practices

- **Daily Planning**: Use the Daily Planner consistently for best results
- **Regular Reviews**: Schedule weekly and monthly reviews of your progress
- **Goal Setting**: Set SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
- **Health Tracking**: Be consistent with health logging for meaningful trends
- **Team Collaboration**: Communicate clearly and set expectations with team members

## Conclusion

Your Qirvo Dashboard is designed to be your comprehensive life management system. Take time to explore all features, customize the experience to your needs, and develop consistent habits for maximum benefit.
