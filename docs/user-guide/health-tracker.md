# Health Tracker

Monitor your physical and mental well-being with comprehensive health tracking.

**Features:**

- Track vital signs and health metrics
- Log symptoms and medications
- Monitor mood and energy levels
- Record exercise and sleep patterns
- Generate health reports and trends

**How to Use:**

1. Access **Health Tracker** from the navigation
2. Add daily health entries
3. Log medications and symptoms
4. Review trends and patterns over time
5. Export data for healthcare providers if needed
